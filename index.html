🔍 Overview Analysis

Researcher Demographics: UC has 68 Scopus-indexed researchers — 55.9% female (38) and 41.2% male (28). The female-majority composition reflects a strong representation of women in research.

Publication Output: Female researchers contribute 59.6% of publications (265) vs. male researchers at 40.4% (180), aligning with the gender distribution of the faculty.

Citation Impact: The FWCI of 0.45 is below world average (1.0), indicating room for growth in citation impact. Female researchers account for 60.5% of total citations (888 of 1468), with a higher average h-index (3.4) compared to male researchers (2.8).

Overall Standing: With 445 total publications and 1,468 citations, UC maintains a growing research portfolio. THE Ranking 1001–1500 reflects institutional standing in global higher education.
